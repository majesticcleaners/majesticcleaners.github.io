Want To Learn More?

The store is called Majestic Cleaners and is on the corner of Gladstone Avenue and Bay Street near downtown of Ottawa, Ontario. This laundry mart has been up and running for more than 10 years and was started by Chan Vien Dam and Phuong Tang from scratch after the used to be famous Hockey Arena was burned down. Which is why at the front of the store there is a hockey monument to have citizens remember the famous hockey rink.

On the side of the laundry mart is a customer parking area. Customers can park there for as long as they wish but non-customers will have their cars towed if they park there. There are also parking spots on the other side throughout Bay Street.
